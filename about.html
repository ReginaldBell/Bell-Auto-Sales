About B & S Auto Sales

B & S Auto Sales is a local, family-run dealership serving Mineola, TX and the surrounding East Texas area. We believe in fair deals, honest service, and treating every customer like a neighbor.

We offer a carefully selected inventory of quality used cars, trucks, and SUVs. Our team is here to answer your questions, help you find the right vehicle, and make the process straightforward—no pressure, no games.

Contact us by phone, email, or our website form. We’re proud to serve Mineola and nearby communities with vehicles you can trust and people you can talk to.
